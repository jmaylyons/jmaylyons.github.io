About

This website would not be complete without addressing my adventures abroad. This past summer, my wander lust brought me To Europe, where I spent a month living with a host family in Malakoff France. I would take two metro lines every morning to reach my classes in Paris. I have been studying la lange d'amour since high school and a chance to jump right into the language was exactly what I needed. I am currently a French minor at UF, and I am pursuing an international relations certificate. The goal is to be fluent. I have been documenting my experience in France with my blog. It's ongoing, so feel free to start reading!
